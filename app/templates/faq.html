Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Gamsuwa and how our platform can help improve HIV client satisfaction.

About Gamsuwa

What is Gamsuwa?

Gamsuwa is an AI-powered platform that predicts and explains HIV client satisfaction based on service interaction data. It uses machine learning and explainable AI to help healthcare providers understand what drives patient satisfaction and improve care quality.

Who developed Gamsuwa?

Gamsuwa was developed by the Africa Hub for Innovation and Development (AHFID), a leading organization focused on leveraging technology and innovation to improve healthcare outcomes across Africa.

What does "Gamsuwa" mean?

Gamsuwa is derived from the Hausa language, meaning "gratitude" or "appreciation," reflecting our commitment to understanding and improving client satisfaction in healthcare settings.

How It Works

How does the prediction model work?

Our model uses CatBoost, a state-of-the-art machine learning algorithm, trained on real-world HIV client satisfaction data. It analyzes multiple factors including demographics, care history, provider interactions, and communication quality to predict satisfaction levels.

What are SHAP values?

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values provide insights into how each feature contributes to the prediction. They help explain which factors had the most positive or negative impact on the predicted satisfaction level.

How accurate is the model?

The predictive model is trained on real-world data and optimized for both accuracy and fairness, achieving an average accuracy of over 85%. However, no prediction is perfect - use it as guidance alongside professional judgment, not as a definitive assessment.

What is the Generative AI explanation?

Our platform uses advanced language models to generate human-readable explanations of predictions, translating technical model outputs into actionable insights that healthcare providers can easily understand and act upon.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes! All inputs are anonymized and never stored or shared. Your privacy is our top priority. We process data in real-time for predictions and discard it immediately after generating results.

Do you collect personal information?

We only collect anonymized, aggregated data for improving our model. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected, stored, or transmitted. The platform is designed with privacy-by-design principles.

Is the platform HIPAA compliant?

While we implement security best practices and privacy safeguards, we recommend that users consult with their legal and compliance teams to ensure the platform meets their specific regulatory requirements.

Using the Platform

How do I make a prediction?

Simply navigate to the Dashboard, fill in the client information form with demographics, care history, and interaction details, then click "Predict & Explain Satisfaction." You'll receive instant results with detailed explanations.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need basic demographic information (age, employment, education), care history (HIV diagnosis duration, facility duration), provider interaction scores (empathy, listening, decision-sharing), and communication assessments.

Can I download or save the results?

Currently, results are displayed on-screen. We recommend taking screenshots or copying the text for your records. We're working on adding export functionality in future updates.

How should I interpret the confidence score?

The confidence score indicates how certain the model is about its prediction. Higher scores (above 70%) suggest more reliable predictions, while lower scores indicate more uncertainty and should be interpreted with additional context.

Best Practices

How can I get the most accurate predictions?

Provide complete and accurate information for all required fields. The more detailed and accurate your input, the more reliable the prediction will be. When uncertain, use the midpoint or most likely value.

Should I use this to replace clinical judgment?

No. Gamsuwa is a decision-support tool designed to augment, not replace, professional judgment. Always combine AI insights with clinical expertise, patient context, and professional experience.

How often is the model updated?

We continuously monitor model performance and update it periodically with new data to ensure accuracy and relevance. Major updates are announced through our communication channels.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gamsuwa?

Gamsuwa is a platform that predicts and explains HIV client satisfaction based on service interaction data.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All inputs are anonymized and never stored or shared. Your privacy is our priority.

Can providers use this tool for training?

Absolutely. Gamsuwa provides explanations that can help improve provider-client communication.

How accurate is the model?

The predictive model is trained on real-world data and optimized for both accuracy and fairness. But no prediction is perfect. Use it as guidance, not gospel.
